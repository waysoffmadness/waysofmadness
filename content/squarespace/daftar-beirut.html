Daftar Beirut : Circus Hub | Episode #01

Paramaz Yepremian is the founder of Beirut Circus Hub, based in Mar Mikhael since 2016. Along with circus workshops and classes, Paramaz is attempting to incorporate new art forms to the circus world. He runs a manufacturing and design atelier for circus toys, and takes part of various circus performances. He organizes free workshops and circus camps, in order to build an alternative circus community in Lebanon, and transform their image of circus, as more than simply a show.
