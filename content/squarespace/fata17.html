FATA17

Sandy chamoun is beirut-based singer and musician better known as one of the founding member of the lebanese group “the great departed”. She is now a member of SANAM, a lebanese group from Lebanon . She released her first solo ep in December 2022 “fata17” . The album is conceptualized  around field recording and sounds from the October 17 revolution in Lebanon.
