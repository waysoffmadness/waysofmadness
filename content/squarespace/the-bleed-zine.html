The Bleed Zine
Photo Serie

Nader deconstructs and examines the intricate layers of religious ritual and human emotion in The Bleed Zine, an ongoing photographic project centered on the Ashura ceremonies in Tyre, south of Lebanon. He questions traditional representations of this significant event, focusing on the collective expressions of devotion, grief, and solidarity.
